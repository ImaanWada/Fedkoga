FGGC Kazaure

FEDKOGA is an association of the girl alumni from the Federal Gorvernment Girls College Kazaure.

About Us


FGGC Kazaure Old Girls Association (FEDKOGA) is an association for the alumni of FGGC Kazaure. Some of the aims and objectives of FEDKOGA are: Planned &sustained nurturing of close relations & cooperation among old students of FGGC Kazaure towards ensuring the school's Development as well as the general welfare of its members.To promote and nurture a close relationship with current students of FGGC and serve as role models to them.

Protection and promotion of the good name of FGGC Kazaure,Fratenizing with other Old students of FGGC Kazaure and Unity Schools Old Associations and advancing unity.Establishment of projects and channeling of proceeds towards development.

Vision

FEDKOGA vision is to support each other and advance the course of Unity in Nigeria.

Mission

Our mission is the Protection and Promotion of the good name and image of Federal Government Girls' College Kazaure.

Core Values

High standards of ethics,conduct,etiquette and discipline.

Our Members


We have 2 categories of members, Full and Honorary members. A full member shall be an old student of Federal Government Girls' College Kazaure, Nigerian Citizen or Expatriate, who has obtained testimonials and /or Certificates from the School and fully subscribes to and is duly registered as a full member of this Association as set out in this constitution. A honorary member shall be any friend of the Association who is admitted as a mark of honour in recognition of his/he service to the Annual General Conference of the Association on recommendation.
